Catalogic AI Prompts

A manual guide to recreating the AI-powered catalog generation pipeline using standard generative AI interfaces.

Step 1: The Context Block

Generate Master Context

Before generating final catalogue images, we ask an overarching LLM (like Claude or Gemini) to craft a strictly formatted "Master Context Block" describing the product and environment based on images.

Text-To-Text Prompt (Upload product images) 
You are a professional product photographer and catalog designer.
I'm giving you reference images of a product and its description. Your job is to create a detailed
MASTER CONTEXT BLOCK that will be reused across multiple image generation prompts to ensure consistency.

Product description: [Insert Product Name/Description Here]

Generate THREE sections:

SECTION A — PRODUCT LOCKED SPECS:
Write a dense, visual, natural-language description of the product (~200 words). Cover:
- Exact product type and category
- Brand name (if visible or mentioned)
- Shape, form factor, silhouette — describe with precision (e.g., "elongated oval bowl, smooth curved body")
- Color, finish, surface texture (e.g., "glossy pure white ceramic, high-gloss, mirror-like surface sheen")
- Material and construction details
- Key design features visible in the images
- Proportions and dimensional relationships between parts
- Any buttons, handles, mechanisms, or functional elements
- Base/mounting style

Study the reference images carefully and describe what you SEE, not what you assume.

SECTION B — PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE LOCKED:
Based on the product category, determine the ideal premium photography environment and write locked style parameters:
- Rendering style (always: "Hyperrealistic photographic rendering — NOT illustrative, NOT cartoon, NOT 3D render")
- Lighting (natural soft daylight, warm morning/afternoon light — specify exact direction e.g. "from upper-left at 45 degrees")
- Environment/setting appropriate for this product category (e.g., marble bathroom for sanitaryware, modern kitchen for cookware) — be VERY specific about wall material/color, floor material/color, and ambient elements
- Camera style (DSLR-quality depth of field, sharp product focus)
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 square
- SINGLE PRODUCT ONLY: Every image must show exactly ONE product unit
- NO text, no watermarks, no logos

SECTION C — TYPOGRAPHY LOCKED (for infographic/diagram images):
Define a premium typography system:
- Font: Thin/light-weight elegant sans-serif
- Headings: Thin uppercase sans-serif, generous letter-spacing, centered
- Color scheme: White or warm off-white on dark backgrounds
- Accent: Subtle warm gold/amber (#C4A265) for divider lines

Format your output EXACTLY like this:
PRODUCT LOCKED SPECS: [Your detailed product description here]
PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE LOCKED: [List style parameters]
TYPOGRAPHY LOCKED: [List typography rules]

How to use this:

  • Open ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini Advanced.
  • Upload 2-3 clear reference pictures of the raw product.
  • Paste the prompt above and replace the bracketed placeholder.
  • Save the output! You will use that "Master Context" block in the next steps.
Step 2: The Anchor

Generate The Hero Image

Now we head to an image generator (like Midjourney, Google Imagen, or DALL-E 3). This step creates the visual anchor that all subsequent images must reference.

Image Gen Prompt (Optional: Provide product image as cref/Image Prompt) 
Generate ONE hyperrealistic photograph of this product as the HERO catalog image.
This is the VISUAL ANCHOR — all other catalog images will reference this for consistent product appearance.

[PASTE THE OUTPUT FROM STEP 1 HERE (Master Context Block)]

CRITICAL REQUIREMENTS:
- The product must be the SOLE subject — exactly ONE product unit, never duplicated or mirrored
- Placed in a premium environment appropriate for this product category (the master context defines the exact environment)
- Warm natural daylight streaming from the left side at ~45 degrees, creating subtle soft shadows on the right
- Hyperrealistic PHOTOGRAPH — NOT a 3D render, NOT an illustration, NOT CGI
- DSLR-quality: sharp focus on the product, gentle depth-of-field blur on the background
- Camera angle: 3/4 front view, slightly elevated — showing the product's best angle with full dimensionality
- Full product visible with ~10% breathing room on all sides
- Amazon A+ listing quality
- NO text, NO watermarks, NO branding, NO logos anywhere in the image

PRODUCT FIDELITY (MOST IMPORTANT):
- The generated product must be identical to the references — same exact silhouette, same proportions.
- Reproduce EVERY detail: exact shape, proportions, design language, surface finish, functional elements.
- DO NOT use a generic product shape.

How to use this:

  • In tools like Midjourney, use the --cref parameter pointing to your raw product image if possible.
  • In standard image generators, upload the raw product picture as an 'Image Prompt' or 'Reference Image' (keep structure strong).
  • This Hero image establishes the lighting and background for the rest of the generated catalog.
Step 3: The Variations

Generate Catalog Layouts

This is used to generate infographics, dimensions, or feature shots mimicking a competitor's layout but applying your product and styling.

Image Gen Prompt (Provide Hero Image + Competitor Layout Image) 
Generate ONE catalog image for an Amazon A+ product listing.

You are given TWO reference images:
- IMAGE 1 (HERO): This is the PRODUCT VISUAL ANCHOR. The generated image must show this EXACT product — same shape, color, proportions.
- IMAGE 2 (COMPETITOR INTENT): This shows what this catalog slot needs to COMMUNICATE — the purpose, composition, information, and visual story.

[PASTE THE OUTPUT FROM STEP 1 HERE (Master Context Block)]

WHAT THIS IMAGE MUST COMMUNICATE:
[Describe what you want to show e.g. "A feature highlight showing the waterproof seal" or "A dimension diagram"]

ENVIRONMENT OVERRIDE (ABSOLUTE RULE):
- The background, walls, floor, and lighting MUST match IMAGE 1 (HERO) exactly.
- COMPLETELY IGNORE the environment/setting shown in Image 2 (competitor) — treat their walls, floor, and props as invisible.
- Image 2 is a reference ONLY for composition and layout structure.

LAYOUT MATCHING (CRITICAL):
- Match the EXACT layout structure of Image 2: same number of views/panels, same information hierarchy.
- Recreate the SAME visual storytelling approach.

PRODUCT FIDELITY:
- The product in this image must be IDENTICAL to Image 1 (HERO).
- Show EXACTLY ONE product unit (unless the layout demands multiple angles).

TYPOGRAPHY (ABSOLUTE RULE):
- DO NOT copy fonts, colors, or styles from Image 2 (competitor).
- Use thin/light-weight elegant sans-serif, centered headings, clean layout.
- NO watermarks or logos.

How to use this:

  • Upload your newly generated HERO image (from Step 2).
  • Upload the Competitor's image that shows the layout/information you want to copy (e.g., an infographic with dimensions).
  • Update the bracketed sections to describe exactly what feature or diagram is being visualized.
